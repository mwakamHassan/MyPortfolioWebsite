Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Plain Yoghurt
  • Garlic, Ginger, salt, cooking oil, onions, tomatoes

Instructions:

  • Wash and soak rice for 20–30 mins. Boil until 70% cooked, then drain.
  • In a sufuria, heat oil, sauté onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until soft.
  • Add meat or veggies and cook until done. Stir in yogurt
  • Layer cooked rice over the meat/vegetable mixture. Add coriander, mint, and a little saffron or color if using.
  • Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 15–20 minutes.
  • Gently mix before serving. Enjoy hot!

Nutritional Info: Calories: ~400–500 kcal Carbs: ~50g Protein: ~20g Fat: ~20g Rich in: Iron, Vitamin B12 (if using meat), and spices with antioxidants
