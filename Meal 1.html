Ugali and Sukuma Wiki

Ingredients:

  • Maize Flour
  • Water
  • Sukuma Wiki
  • Garlic, salt, cooking oil, onions, tomatoes

Instructions:

  • In a sufuria, boil water. Gradually add maize flour while stirring to avoid lumps. Keep stirring until thick and firm. Let it steam for a few minutes, then set aside
  • In a sufuria, heat oil and sauté onions (and garlic if desired) until soft. Add tomatoes and cook until they form a sauce.
  • Add chopped sukuma wiki, stir, and cook for 5–7 minutes until tender. Add salt to taste.
  • Serve the sukuma wiki with a portion of hot ugali.

Nutritional Info: Calories: ~270 kcal Carbs: ~45g Protein: ~5g Fat: ~5.5g Fiber: ~6g Rich in: Vitamin A, C, K, Iron, Calcium, and Folate
